Swing Catalyst App

Swing Catalyst App

Interaction design and graphical design
Initial Force AS 2013

An iOS app for golf swing analysis.

Product link    |    App Store link

Record your golf swing

Automatic highest possible frame rate when recording. Timer function for recording yourself. Aim guides for all angles. Automatic video trimming before and after impact.

Analyse your golf swing

Drawing tools, frame stepping and zoom to highlight and analyze your technique. Side by side video comparison with other golf swings.

Sharing

Share videos and communicate with others via Online or other social media.

Content sale

Videos of professional tour players available for purchase in the app.

Fan pages

Information about your favourite tour player.

Eco-system

The Swing Catalyst app is connected to the Online webportal and the Swing Catalyst software via the Online cloud service. Videos and communication can be shared directly from the app to others with an Online account.
