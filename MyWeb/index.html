Leeway

UX design, emotions and ability

Close

In-depth interviews

Identify the emotional responses connected to the use of a camera and shooting photos. I interviewed 8 people between 20-34 years old.

The conlusion:
Both genders shows a desire to take good photos. A technical advanced appearance may discourage those without a technical interest. However the desire to be percieved by others as a skilled photographer, may still be present. Good photos gives pride and enables the desire to evolve. It's important to trigger the ability to capture good photos early to ensure continued use.

Shape associations test

Identify the emotional responses associated with holding and touching shapes. I tested 8 people between 20-27 years old.

The conlusion:
Safe to hold was an important emotion: "I don't want to drop my camera." Many of the users wanted a camera that gives credibility as someone interested in photography, but shouldn't be too advanced to use.

Philosophy

With a good lens and interesting angles it is easier to shoot good photos. The camera must suppert the most common lense mount, to provide access to a wide selection of good lenses. A cylindrical camera extended by the lense, creates a tubular shape which makes it easy to control the direction you are shooting. Bluetooth connection between phone and camera encourage the photographer to shoot from other interesting angles.

Genoa

Master thesis - Industrial design engineering

Close

The road towards production

Master thesis - Industrial design engineering Norwegian University of Technology and Science 2012

Genoa is a lounge chair for homes and public areas, expanding the Spinnaker series. The goal of the thesis was to bring the chair from concept to production. The project was in cooperation with Spinnaker Norway AS.

prototype

A prototype was built to test both the concept and production methods. Real methods were used in collaboration with potential sub-contractors.

production

Alot of attention was given to the choice of materials and assembly of the chair. Stringers made of spring steel for a comfy feel, held together by brackets and steel tubes. The sailcloth is strapped to a steel tube seat frame, using industrial velcro for easy assembly. The pillow has a soft outer layers for comfort and a firm core for stability. Split into three parts with extra padding in the middle part for improved lumbar support.

WordFindR

UX/UI design, word puzzle game

Close

The project

This is a spare time project in collaboration with some friends. We had this simple idea about a word puzzle game, and created a prototype to test the game mechanics. It turned out to be a quite fast and engaging game between to people. So we decided to develop the game and try to get it published. We are still working on the last part, but soon maybe you too can try it.

Layout and code

The app is built with Xamarin and the MvvmCross framework. I did the UI design and the layout coding. For the iOS app I built the layouts in Xcode using auto layout for adaptive behaviour. Layouts for Android I coded in Visual Studio, for both phone and tablet size screens. My friends did all the magic bits and pieces that makes the app actually work.
