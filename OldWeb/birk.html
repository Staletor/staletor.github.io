Birk - a Scandinavian fireplace tool set

School group project
Norwegian University of Technology and Science 2009

Anders Kjøllesdal
Gregoire Bonnemaire
Ståle Torger Stokke

the concept

A modern fireplace tool set in Scandinavian style. Based on traditional crafts. Made in high pressure laminate and steel. On assignment from Norsk Flid Husfliden.

physical model

The model was built with laminate, steel and foam. Traditional craft methods were used on the laminate and the steel.

Birk

The Birk fireplace tool set consist of three tools: poker, broom and shovel.
Birk also comes with a firewood basket made in high pressure laminate.
In addition a shop display concept was presented for use in the Husfliden stores.
