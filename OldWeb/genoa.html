Genoa lounge chair - the road towards production

Genoa lounge chair - the road towards production

Master thesis - Industrial design engineering
Norwegian University of Technology and Science 2012

A lounge chair for the home and public areas. From concept to production.
An expansion of the Spinnaker series by Hødnebø. Master thesis in cooperation with Spinnaker Norway AS.

the origin

The Genoa concept was originally a product of the Summer Design Office in 2011. The concept was developed in collaboration with Anders Kjøllesdal commissioned by Hødnebø AS.

master thesis

The goal of my master thesis was to have the Genoa ready for production. My focus was on fine tuning the dimension, material selection and production methods in collaboration with Hødnebø and potential sub-contractors.

prototype

A prototype was built to test both the concept and the production methods. Realistic production methods and sub-contractors were used.

Genoa - ready for production?

Although a prototype was made in collaboration with sub-contractors and by realistic methods Genoa is not ready for production. The prototype lacks the refinement required in a quality furniture, and the steel spring stringers have too many production challenges. A second and probably a third iteration of production methods and prototyping is necessary before this chair is ready for production.

the frame

Stringers made of powder coated spring steel and held together by brackets and steel tubes. The spring steel stringers gives the chair a rocking ability.

the seat

Sailcloth strapped to a steel tube seat frame. Industrial velcro used for easy assembly.

the comfort

The pillow has soft outer layers for comfort and a firm core for stability. Split into three parts with extra padding in middle part for better lumbar support.
